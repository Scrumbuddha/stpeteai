About St. Pete AI

St. Pete AI is a vibrant community dedicated to advancing artificial intelligence knowledge and innovation in the Tampa Bay area. We bring together professionals, students, researchers, and enthusiasts who share a passion for AI and machine learning.

Workshops, tutorials, and presentations covering the latest in AI, from fundamentals to cutting-edge research.

Our Mission

To foster a collaborative environment where individuals can explore artificial intelligence, share knowledge, and drive innovation that benefits our community and beyond.

  • check_circle Democratize AI education and resources
  • check_circle Support local AI startups and projects
  • check_circle Promote ethical and responsible AI development
  • check_circle Bridge academia and industry in the Tampa Bay region

Our Vision

To establish St. Petersburg as a leading hub for AI innovation and talent, creating opportunities for growth, discovery, and positive impact through artificial intelligence.
